About

How Pleasantville Design came to be.

  • 1985

    Our Humble Beginnings

    My daughter Becca was born and I started to get into the world of crafting!

  • June 2010

    Discovered my passion for wedding decor

    I got married to the love of my life and uncovered my gift of creating unique decoration and flower arrangments.

  • Febuary 2017

    Created Pleasantville Design

    Started the facebook page, helping brides, and creating one of a kind pieces!
